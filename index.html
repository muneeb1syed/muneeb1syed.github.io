Projects

Making A Workbench With CAD

I made this workbench to be an extension for my desk, I designed it to hold heavy weight at the top and be easily stored underneath my desk when not in use.
CAD files And Blueprints Included!.

A Prosthetic Arm With A Contactless Control System

I created a prosthetic arm capable of movement controlled through gestures made by a user's arm. These gestures are registered using computer vision in a non-invasive manner

Detecting the Enviorment for Visually Impaired Indivisuals

One of my early Arduino projects was to create a device that detected obstacles in the enviorment. Using haptic feedback, the user is be alerted to their surroundings. This device would be most helpful to individuals with visual impairment
Fritzing Diagram and Program Files Included!

Harnessing IoT to Autonomously Alert Emergency Services

Designing a small wearable capable of instantaneously alerting medical personnel when the wearer is in a state of critical health.
