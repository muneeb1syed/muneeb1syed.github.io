Interfaith Thanksgiving Speech



At the Saint Johns Interfaith Prayer service, I gave a reflection on how my religion shows unity for all people and pushes me to strive for equality Each year, Saint John's holds an Interfaith Prayer service and invites students to give a reflection discussing their religion. The reflection is listened to by the nearly 1000 students and 50 or so staff members. I first gave a speech in my sophomore year in 2018 discussing how Muslims show thankfulness in the context of religion. More recently, in 2020, I discussed the concept of unity in Islam and how Muslims across the globe come together in harmony.
