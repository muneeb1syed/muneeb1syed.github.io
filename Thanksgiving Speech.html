Interfaith Thanksgiving Speech

Each year, Saint John's holds an Interfaith Prayer service and invites students to give a reflection discussing their religion. The reflection is listend to by the nearly 900 students and 50 or so staff members. I first gave a speech in my sophomore year in 2018 discussing how Muslims show thankfulness in the context of religion. More recently, in 2020, I discussed the concept of unity in Islam and how Muslims across the globe come together in harmony
